Body shaping on freezing fat (cryolipolysis)

The outstanding feature of fat cell removal is that fat bulges are targeted using a spray of cooling treatment. Gradually the fat cells are eliminated over a two to four month period. The procedure reduces fat in a chosen, targeted part of the body, such as the love handles, back, stomach, or bottom. This method does not require surgery, there is no anesthesia, no needles, no cuts, and it only targets fat cells without harming the skin or any other tissue.
